Welcome to the Cinema

Cinema is the art of telling stories using moving images, sound, music, and emotions. It entertains people and connects people emotionally.

What is Script?

A script is the written plan of a movie, drama, or video.

What is Screenplay?

A screenplay shows what we SEE and HEAR on screen.

Types of Screenplay

Screenplay Type Definition
Linear Screenplay Story straight order-la pogum (start → middle → end)
Non-Linear Screenplay Past & present mix pannuvaanga
Hyperlink Screenplay Multiple stories connected through theme
Rashomon Style Same incident shown from different POV
Real-Time Screenplay Movie time ≈ story time
Reverse Screenplay Story ending-la start aagi past-ku pogum
Single Character Driven One main character follow pannum
Experimental Screenplay Traditional cinema rules break pannuvaanga
